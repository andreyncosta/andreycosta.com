03 ——

Research

FGV EPPG · Master's Thesis in progress

The Bitdollar Hypothesis:
Bitcoin & the Dollar as
Co-Sovereign Stores of Value

What happens to the international monetary system if Bitcoin becomes a sovereign reserve asset alongside — not instead of — the US dollar? This research examines the macroeconomic and institutional implications of a "bitdollar" adoption scenario: the co-existence of BTC and USD as parallel stores of value at the state level, and the structural tensions this creates for fiscal policy, seigniorage, and monetary sovereignty in emerging economies.
